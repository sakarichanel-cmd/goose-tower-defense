# Goose TD - Advanced Strategy Guide

## Winning Strategy Framework

### Early Game (Waves 1-3)
- Build a balanced defense with Archer Towers
- Focus on covering the entire map
- Save resources for mid-game upgrades
- Don't over-invest in single towers

### Mid Game (Waves 4-7)
- Transition to mixed tower strategies
- Upgrade high-value positions
- Start saving for Cannon Towers
- Identify bottleneck areas and reinforce them

### Late Game (Waves 8+)
- Use all tower types strategically
- Focus fire on boss enemies
- Maintain resource reserves
- Adapt to new enemy patterns

## Map Control

1. **Identify choke points** - Where enemies must pass
2. **Create kill zones** - Overlapping tower coverage
3. **Secondary defense** - Backup towers near your base
4. **Buffer zone** - Towers far enough to catch early arrivals

## Economic Management

- **Coins per wave:** Plan spending around incoming coins
- **Upgrade priority:** Health > Damage > Range
- **Emergency savings:** Always keep 50+ coins for repairs
- **Risk vs. Reward:** Balance offense and defense

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Placing all towers in one area
❌ Upgrading weak towers
❌ Ignoring boss waves
❌ Spending all resources at once
❌ Not adjusting to new enemy types

## Advanced Tactics

### The Funnel Strategy
Create a narrow path forcing enemies through overlapping tower fire.

### The Ring Defense
Place towers in concentric circles around your base for maximum coverage.

### The Swarm Breaker
Use Lightning Towers to damage multiple enemies simultaneously.
