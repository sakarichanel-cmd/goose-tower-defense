## Game Objective

Defend your goose nest against waves of enemies! Place towers strategically to eliminate all enemies before they reach your base.

## Controls

- **Click on tiles** to place towers
- **Drag to move** towers around the map
- **Right-click** to remove towers
- **Space bar** to pause/unpause
- **Speed buttons** to control game speed

## Tower Types

### 🏹 Archer Tower
- **Cost:** 100 coins
- **Damage:** Medium
- **Range:** Wide
- **Speed:** Fast
- **Best for:** General defense

### ⚡ Lightning Tower
- **Cost:** 150 coins
- **Damage:** High
- **Range:** Medium
- **Speed:** Slow
- **Best for:** Multiple enemies

### 🔥 Cannon Tower
- **Cost:** 200 coins
- **Damage:** Very High
- **Range:** Medium
- **Speed:** Very Slow
- **Best for:** Bosses and strong enemies

## Enemy Types

- **Goblins:** Fast and weak
- **Orcs:** Slow and strong
- **Dragons:** Very fast and dangerous
- **Bosses:** Rare, extremely powerful

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Plan ahead** - Anticipate enemy paths and tower placement
2. **Upgrade strategically** - Focus on high-traffic areas
3. **Mix towers** - Combine different tower types for better coverage
4. **Use terrain** - Place towers on elevated areas when possible
5. **Manage resources** - Don't spend all your coins at once
