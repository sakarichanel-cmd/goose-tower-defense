# Goose TD - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: Is Goose TD free to play?
**A:** Yes! Goose TD is completely free and plays in your browser with no downloads required.

### Q: Do I need an account to play?
**A:** No account is required. Just visit the game link and start playing immediately!

### Q: What devices can I play on?
**A:** Goose TD works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers. Performance is best on desktop.

### Q: Can I save my progress?
**A:** Progress is saved locally in your browser. Clearing your browser data may erase saved games.

## Gameplay Questions

### Q: What's the difference between difficulty levels?
**A:** Higher difficulties feature more enemies, faster waves, and reduced income.

### Q: Can I restart a level?
**A:** Yes, use the restart button to begin the current wave again.

### Q: What happens if an enemy reaches my base?
**A:** You lose one life. Lose all lives and it's game over!

### Q: How do I unlock new towers?
**A:** New towers become available as you progress through waves.

## Technical Questions

### Q: Why is the game lagging?
**A:** Close other browser tabs, reduce your browser's zoom level, or try a different browser.

### Q: Does Goose TD work offline?
**A:** No, an internet connection is required to play.

### Q: What browser should I use?
**A:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge all work well. Use the latest version for best performance.

### Q: Can I play on my phone?
**A:** Yes! Mobile support is fully optimized, though a larger screen is recommended.

## Account & Progress

### Q: How do I upload my high score?
**A:** High scores are stored locally. Share screenshots in the [discussions](https://github.com/sakarichanel-cmd/goose-tower-defense/discussions)!

### Q: Can I play with friends?
**A:** Single-player mode is currently available. Multiplayer is planned for future updates!

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The game won't load
**A:** Try refreshing the page, clearing your cache, or trying a different browser.

### Q: My towers won't place
**A:** Check that you have enough coins and the tile isn't already occupied.

### Q: Why is the game so slow?
**A:** Reduce game speed using the speed controls or close other applications.
